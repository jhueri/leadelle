What is Leadelle?

Leadelle is a platform dedicated to spotlighting student-led initiatives founded by girls and young women. Our mission is to empower these youth founders by giving their projects the visibility they deserve. We help them connect with a supportive community, access valuable resources, and share their ideas with the world.

At Leadelle, we believe in the power of young women to lead boldly and create change. Through our platform, we provide the tools and opportunities to inspire, grow, and make a lasting impact. Together, we’re building a space where every girl with a big idea has the chance to shine.

Why I Built Leadelle

Felicity Tabia

I started Leadelle because I wanted to create the platform I wish existed when I launched my first initiative—one that uplifts girls with big ideas and gives them the recognition they deserve.
