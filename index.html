What is Leadelle?

Leadelle is a platform dedicated to spotlighting student-led initiatives founded by girls and young women. Our mission is to empower these youth founders by giving their projects the visibility they deserve.

We also help connect student initiatives with volunteering and internship opportunities. Many of the projects featured on Leadelle are seeking interns or volunteers, making it a great resource for anyone looking for an internship or volunteering opportunity for high school students or college students.

At Leadelle, we believe in the power of young women to lead boldly and create change. Through our platform, we provide the tools, community, and support to inspire, grow, and make a lasting impact.

Submit Your Initiative

By submitting your initiative, you’ll gain visibility, connect with a supportive network, and have the chance to recruit volunteers or interns. Your project could become a hub for internship opportunities or volunteering for high school students and college students looking to get involved and grow their skills.
