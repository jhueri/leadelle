For the Girl Building Something Bigger

Leadelle is where female student founders grow, connect, and get the spotlight they deserve.

Our Mission

Leadelle was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to support the next generation of female student founders by giving them the recognition, resources, and real support they need to lead. Whether you’re building a nonprofit, a tech tool, a campaign, or something that doesn’t even have a name yet. Leadelle exists to make sure you’re not doing it alone.

We're building a vibrant community where passionate changemakers can share resources, find inspiration, and collaborate across disciplines. While currently US-based, we dream of a future where Leadelle supports emerging female leaders worldwide.
