Dreamers Avenue

Dreamers Avenue is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering undocumented and first-generation students. We provide resources like mentorship, scholarships, and skill-building opportunities to help students achieve their academic, professional, and personal goals. Our mission is to create a supportive community where every student feels seen, supported, and unstoppable.

Safe Internet 4 Me

This website was created for students, by a student. The main goal of this website was to make cybersecurity and digital safety resources easy to access and understand. This website hosts some cybersecurity resources that have helped me, on top of resources that I have created of my own. To make cybersecurity fun, I have even made my own cybersecurity content that consist of Minecraft that breaks down cybersecurity concepts into literal blocks!

NCLS Foundation

Since 2023, the NCLS Foundation initiative has completed three annual creative projects, donating over 7,000 artworks to the Students Rebuild program—formerly managed by the Bezos Family Foundation and now managed by Creative Visions Foundation—and unlocking a five-figure sum to support their partners, including UNICEF, the National Wildlife Federation, Choose Earth, Eden Reforestation Projects, and Little Amal.

Health Beyond Borders

Health Beyond Borders (HBB) is a youth-led nonprofit dedicated to improving global health literacy through culturally relevant education. We create engaging children’s books, health kits, and digital resources to empower underserved communities. By hosting events, publishing materials, and collaborating with other organizations, HBB fosters awareness, prevention, and advocacy. Our mission is to make health education accessible, inclusive, and impactful for young people worldwide.

Heroic Hearts

Heroic Hearts is a bold, youth-led movement spotlighting everyday heroes—the unrecognized changemakers all around us. Through powerful interviews, we honor those making a difference with heart, courage, and purpose. Because heroes don’t wear capes—they walk among us. And who knows? You might be next. Ready to be seen? Heroic Hearts is where your story becomes someone else’s inspiration.
